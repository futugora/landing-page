Privacy Policy for KeyBard

Last Updated: August 26, 2024

Thank you for using KeyBard ("the App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we handle your data. The short version is: we don't collect any of it.

1. No Data Collection

KeyBard is designed with your privacy as a priority. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information about you. We do not have user accounts, and we do not use any third-party analytics or advertising frameworks.

2. MIDI and Audio Processing

All MIDI and audio data processed by KeyBard happens in real-time, exclusively on your device. This data is never stored, recorded, or transmitted off your machine by the App.

3. Third-Party Audio Unit Plugins

KeyBard acts as a host for Audio Unit plugins that are already installed on your system. The App interacts with these plugins to produce sound based on your MIDI input. KeyBard does not send any personal information to these plugins. Your use of third-party plugins is subject to the privacy policies of their respective developers.

4. Configuration Data

To improve your experience, KeyBard stores some non-personal configuration settings locally on your device using the standard macOS `UserDefaults` system. This includes:

  • The identifier of the last-used Audio Unit plugin and preset.
  • Your preference for whether the App should launch at login.

This data is stored only on your device, is not linked to you personally, and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this space. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
